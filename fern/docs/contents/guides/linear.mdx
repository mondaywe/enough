#### Obtaining Linear Client ID and Secret

-   Open a [Linear account](https://linear.app/signup).
-   Create a Linear app [here](https://linear.app/revertinc/settings/api/applications/new), more auth details mentioned [here](https://developers.linear.app/docs/oauth/authentication#id-1.-create-an-oauth2-application-in-linear)
    -   `NOTE:` You can skip this step and use the default revert Linear app
-   Set `https://app.revert.dev/oauth-callback/linear` as the `Callback Urls` url for your app
-   **Get your client_id and client_secret**:
    -   Click on the Profile Picture, go to `Settings`->`API` (under `My Account` section) -> below `OAuth Applications` click on `...` of your configured app -> click `Manage application`
    -   Copy your client_id and client_secret

#### Connect to Linear via Revert

-   Create an account on Revert if you don't already have one. (https://app.revert.dev/sign-up)
-   Login to your revert dashboard (https://app.revert.dev/sign-in) and click on `Customize your apps` - `Linear`

<img src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dfcnic8wq/image/upload/v1705652583/Revert/Docs/nwlrrom2sw5xj7dsgznv.png" />

-   Enter the `client_id` and `client_secret` you copied in the previous step into the App credentials here and click `Submit`.
