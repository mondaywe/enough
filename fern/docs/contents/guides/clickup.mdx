#### Obtaining Clickup Client ID and Secret

-   Open a [Clickup](https://app.clickup.com/signup).
-   Click on avatar icon, go to `Settings` -> `ClickUp API`
-   Create a Clickup app, using the steps mentioned [here](https://clickup.com/api/developer-portal/authentication/#step-1-create-an-oauth-app)
    -   `NOTE:` You can skip this step and use the default revert Clickup app
-   Set `https://app.revert.dev/oauth-callback/clickup` as the `redirect` url for your app
-   **Get your client_id and client_secret**:
    -   Go to the "ClickUp API" click on the app
    -   Copy your client_id and client_secret

#### Connect to Clickup via Revert

-   Create an account on Revert if you don't already have one. (https://app.revert.dev/sign-up)
-   Login to your revert dashboard (https://app.revert.dev/sign-in) and click on `Customize your apps` - `Clickup`

<img src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dfcnic8wq/image/upload/v1705652583/Revert/Docs/nwlrrom2sw5xj7dsgznv.png" />

-   Enter the `client_id` and `client_secret` you copied in the previous step into the App credentials here and click `Submit`.
