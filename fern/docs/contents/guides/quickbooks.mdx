#### Obtaining QuickBooks Client ID and Secret

-   Open a [QuickBooks developer account](https://accounts.intuit.com/app/sign-in).
-   Create a QuickBooks OAuth consumer, using the steps mentioned [here](https://developer.intuit.com/app/developer/qbo/docs/develop/authentication-and-authorization/oauth-2.0)
    -   `NOTE:` You can skip this step and use the default revert QuickBooks app
-   Set `https://app.revert.dev/oauth-callback/quickbooks` as the `redirect` url for your app
-   **Get your client_id and client_secret**:

#### Connect to QuickBooks via Revert

-   Create an account on Revert if you don't already have one. (https://app.revert.dev/sign-up)
-   Login to your revert dashboard (https://app.revert.dev/sign-in) and go to Integrations section and click on `Create App` - `QuickBooks`

<img src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dfcnic8wq/image/upload/v1705652583/Revert/Docs/nwlrrom2sw5xj7dsgznv.png" />

-   Enter the `client_id` and `client_secret` you copied in the previous step into the App credentials here and click `Submit`.
