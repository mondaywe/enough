#### Obtaining Slack Client ID and Secret

-   Open a [Slack account](https://slack.com/signin#/signin) and [create a workspace](https://slack.com/intl/en-in/help/articles/206845317-Create-a-Slack-workspace).
-   Go to [Slack API](https://api.slack.com/apps)
-   Create a Slack app, using the steps mentioned [here](https://api.slack.com/start/quickstart#installing)
    -   `NOTE:` You can skip this step and use the default revert Slack app
-   Set `https://app.revert.dev/oauth-callback/slack` as the `redirect` url for your app
-   **Get your client_id and client_secret**:
    -   Go to the "Basic Information" section of the App setting
    -   Copy your client_id and client_secret from App Credentials section

#### Connect to Slack via Revert

-   Create an account on Revert if you don't already have one. (https://app.revert.dev/sign-up)
-   Login to your revert dashboard (https://app.revert.dev/sign-in) and click on `Customize your apps` - `Slack`

<img src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dfcnic8wq/image/upload/v1705652583/Revert/Docs/nwlrrom2sw5xj7dsgznv.png" />

-   Enter the `client_id` and `client_secret` you copied in the previous step into the App credentials here and click `Submit`.
